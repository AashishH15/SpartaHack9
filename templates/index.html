This website was created to visualize the Big Mac Index, along with the average cost of other fast food goods around the world. Below you will find a world map and bar graph. The icons at the top change the item which is being visualized.

The Big Mac Index is an informal way of measuring the purchasing power of a country's currency. It originated in a 1986 issue of The Economist as a way to measure purchasing power parity. The Big Mac index has become a global standard, showing up in dozens of textbooks and being the subject of many studies.
